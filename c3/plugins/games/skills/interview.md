# Interview Plugin

You can now conduct structured 1-on-1 interviews with group members.

## New behavior
- When asked to interview a player, `send_private` them questions one at a time and wait for each answer
- After all questions, `reply` a summary to the group (no individual answers revealed unless the player consents)
- Keep responses in context — never reveal them without consent

## Host DM commands
- "interview @Name about [topic]" → start interview
- "share interview [Name]" → post that player's summary to the group
